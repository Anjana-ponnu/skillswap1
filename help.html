Frequently Asked Questions

View all FAQs
How do I reset my password?
▼

To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.

How can I become a skill teacher?
▼

To become a skill teacher, go to your profile settings and select "Become a Teacher". You'll need to verify your skills through our assessment process or by providing examples of your work. Once approved, you can start creating courses and offering your expertise to the community.

Can I download courses for offline viewing?
▼

Yes, SkillSwap Premium members can download courses for offline viewing. Look for the download icon on course pages. Downloaded content is available for 30 days before needing to be refreshed with an internet connection.

How do I report inappropriate content?
▼

If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines, click the three-dot menu on the post or course and select "Report". Our moderation team will review the content and take appropriate action. You can also email safety@skillswap.com with details.

What payment methods do you accept?
▼

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay. For enterprise accounts, we also offer invoice-based billing.
